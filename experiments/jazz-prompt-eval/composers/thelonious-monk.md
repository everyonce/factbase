# Thelonious Monk

## Overview

- Born: October 10, 1917, Rocky Mount, North Carolina @t[=1917] [^1]
- Died: February 17, 1982, Englewood, New Jersey @t[=1982] [^1]
- Primary instrument: piano @t[?]
- Second most recorded jazz composer after Duke Ellington @t[?] [^2]

## Career

- Developed his distinctive style at Minton's Playhouse @t[1940..1944] [^3]
- First recordings as leader for Blue Note @t[=1947] [^4]
- Columbia Records period, peak commercial success @t[1962..1970] [^5]
- Largely retired from performing @t[1973..1982] [^1]

## Compositional Style

- Angular melodies with unexpected intervals @t[?]
- Dissonant harmonies and "wrong note" aesthetic @t[?]
- Silence used as compositional element @t[?]

## Key Compositions

- Round Midnight @t[=1944] [^6]
- Straight, No Chaser @t[=1951] [^4]
- Blue Monk @t[=1954] [^4]
- Epistrophy @t[=1942] [^7]
