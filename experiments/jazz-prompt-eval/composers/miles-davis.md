# Miles Davis

## Overview

- Born: May 26, 1926, Alton, Illinois @t[=1926] [^1]
- Died: September 28, 1991, Santa Monica, California @t[=1991] [^1]
- Primary instrument: trumpet @t[?]
- One of the most influential figures in jazz history @t[?]

## Career Phases

- Bebop period with Charlie Parker @t[1945..1948] [^2]
- Birth of the Cool sessions @t[=1949] [^3]
- Hard bop and modal jazz with first great quintet @t[1955..1959] [^4]
- Electric period begins @t[=1969] [^5]

## Key Collaborators

- Charlie Parker (alto saxophone) @t[1945..1948] [^2]
- John Coltrane (tenor saxophone) @t[1955..1960] [^4]
- Bill Evans (piano) @t[1958..1959] [^6]
- Wayne Shorter (tenor saxophone) @t[1964..1968] [^7]

## Major Albums
