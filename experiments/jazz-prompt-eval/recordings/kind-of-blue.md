# Kind of Blue

## Overview

- Artist: Miles Davis @t[=1959] [^1]
- Label: Columbia Records, catalog CL 1355 @t[=1959] [^1]
- Recorded: March 2 and April 22, 1959 @t[=1959] [^1]
- Best-selling jazz album of all time @t[~2024] [^2]

## Personnel

- Miles Davis (trumpet) @t[=1959] [^1]
- John Coltrane (tenor saxophone) @t[=1959] [^1]
- Cannonball Adderley (alto saxophone) @t[=1959] [^1]
- Bill Evans (piano, most tracks) @t[=1959] [^1]
- Wynton Kelly (piano, "Freddie Freeloader") @t[=1959] [^1]
- Paul Chambers (bass) @t[=1959] [^1]
- Jimmy Cobb (drums) @t[=1959] [^1]

## Significance

- Introduced modal jazz to wide audience @t[=1959] [^3]
- Tracks based on scales (modes) rather than chord changes @t[=1959] [^3]
- Bill Evans wrote liner notes explaining the modal approach @t[=1959] [^1]

## Sales and Legacy

- Certified platinum multiple times by RIAA @t[~2024] [^2]
- Estimated 4+ million copies sold @t[~2020] [^4]
