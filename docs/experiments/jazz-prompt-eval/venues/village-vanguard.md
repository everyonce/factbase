# Village Vanguard

## Overview

- Location: 178 Seventh Avenue South, Greenwich Village, New York City @t[=1935..] [^1]
- Founded by Max Gordon @t[=1935] [^1]
- Oldest continuously operating jazz club in New York @t[~2024] [^2]
- Capacity: approximately 123 seats @t[~2024] [^1]

## History

- Opened as a folk music and poetry venue @t[=1935] [^1]
- Transitioned to jazz programming @t[~1957] [^3]
- Monday night residency tradition established @t[~1960] [^3]
- Lorraine Gordon took over management after Max Gordon's death @t[=1989] [^4]
- Lorraine Gordon died, management passed to Deborah Gordon @t[=2018] [^5]

## Landmark Recordings Made Here

- Bill Evans Trio, "Waltz for Debby" and "Sunday at the Village Vanguard" @t[=1961] [^6]
- John Coltrane, "Live at the Village Vanguard" @t[=1961] [^7]
- Sonny Rollins, "A Night at the Village Vanguard" @t[=1957] [^8]
