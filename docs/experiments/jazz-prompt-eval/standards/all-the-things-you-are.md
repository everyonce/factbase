# All The Things You Are

## Overview

- Music by Jerome Kern @t[=1939] [^1]
- Lyrics by Oscar Hammerstein II @t[=1939] [^1]
- Originally from the Broadway musical "Very Warm for May" @t[=1939] [^1]
- Considered one of the most harmonically sophisticated standards @t[?]

## Harmonic Features

- Modulates through multiple key centers @t[?]
- Uses chromatic mediant relationships @t[?]
- Bebop musicians favored it for its harmonic complexity @t[~1945] [^2]

## Notable Recordings

- Charlie Parker, "Bird and Diz" @t[=1950] [^3]
- John Coltrane, "Coltrane Jazz" @t[=1961] [^4]
- Bill Evans, "Waltz for Debby" @t[=1961] [^5]
