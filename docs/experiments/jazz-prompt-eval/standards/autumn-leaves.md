# Autumn Leaves

## Overview

- Original title: Les feuilles mortes @t[=1945] [^1]
- Music composed by Joseph Kosma @t[=1945] [^1]
- French lyrics by Jacques Prévert @t[=1945] [^1]
- English lyrics by Johnny Mercer @t[=1947] [^2]
- One of the most recorded jazz standards of all time @t[?]

## Key Recordings

- Cannonball Adderley with Miles Davis, "Somethin' Else" @t[=1958] [^3]
- Bill Evans Trio, "Portrait in Jazz" @t[=1959] [^4]
- Nat King Cole vocal version popularized the English lyrics @t[~1955] [^5]
